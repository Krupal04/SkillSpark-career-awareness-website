SkillsSpark

About SkillsSpark

SkillsSpark is a career-awareness web project created as a semester assignment. It helps learners explore careers and skills and provides an interactive quiz to help suggest fit.

Mission

To make career information accessible, actionable, and engaging so that students can take confident steps toward their future.
